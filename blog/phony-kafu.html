この世で造花より綺麗な花は無いわ

A little appreciation to my favourite artist.

As you know, this website, kafu.ovh, is a reference to KAF from Kamitsubaki Studio.

Listening to her covers and original songs always hits me right in the feels. Her silking smooth voice yet brittle is so unique, she’s like the opposite of Ado.

It really is something when the producer itself, [tweet here], don’t even know how KAF got big in the first place.

I mean, not really; it’s obvious.

Just listen to KAF, and you’ll get it. She’s just that good.

It’s a bit funny how they have to defend themselves just because KAF got so big, do note that she’s 14 at this point in time, and she’s already this good, it’s just amazing.

One of my favorite songs, “Goodbye for Now”, is the 3rd cover KAF ever did in her YouTube channel, isn’t that crazy? -and oh my god, the cover is so good, I can’t really explain it, since it’s something emotional, but holy hell, I do cry sometimes listening to this. There’s her other songs, These are my favourites.

You can’t miss with KAF and KAFU (Her, but in voice synthesizer form, something like Vocaloid), all of her songs is just good.

And I’m so glad, that the company didn’t try to squeeze out her every worth for money, you can tell that they really care about her.
