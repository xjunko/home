biography

Wow, look who decided to show up, -is what I would say, but I doubt anyone would read this. Anyways, you can call me Junko, I'm the one who made this website. I'm currently a student that's been making whatever I feel like making. I'm not really good at anything, but I'm trying my best to improve.

Also, Isn't it funny that you can just be anyone on the internet?

I’ve been programming since I was a child - probably not the healthiest hobby growing up, but hey, here I am years later, still doing it. Back then, I spent most of my time making absolutely awful games in Clickteam Fusion. Eventually, I moved on to WordPress, where my proudest creation was a clone of osu!'s Circle People skins website. I don’t have it anymore (and honestly, that might be for the best), but it was fun while it lasted.

Sometime around late 2018, I started learning Python - mainly because I wanted to make my own version of the osu! owo bot. If you can’t tell by now, I have a habit of making clones of things I like. It’s honestly one of the best ways to learn, I think.

Here are some quick facts about me:
  • I'm a big fan of Rhythm Games, here are some that I played quite frequently:
    • maimai DX
    • CHUNITHM
    • Project Sekai
    • osu!
  • My favourite Voice Synth is KAF, if you can't tell already from the website.
  • I also like listening to Music, recently I've been listening to [NOMELON NOLEMON], especially the ルール album!

projects

Listed below are my projects that I think are worth sharing...
  • flower-rs - an experimental unix-like kernel written in Rust.
  • moete-rs - a community discord bot, mostly used for custom global emotes and utilities, it is my most longest running project.
  • kurarin - a from scratch implementation of the osu!standard gamemode, written in an experimental language, V; fully supports .osu and .osr replay file format!
I think that should be enough to show what kind of projects I usually work on, if you want to see more of my projects, you can check out my GitHub!
